Description of This Page

    Once a week the National Security Agency releases a public cryptography challenge in the form of a standard substitution cipher, where they track the time it takes participants to complete the puzzle.

    Unfortunately, substitution ciphers are not really good challenges for the crypto community, so, to improve the NSA's challenge, this page is generated by a tool that automatically solves it.

    Please feel free to share the link with as many people as you think would care.
